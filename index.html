ריטיינר שמשקף שותפות, דמי השמה שמשקפים תוצאה

ריטיינר חודשי לכל משרה פתוחהמכסה את העבודה השוטפת בתוך הצוות: אפיון עם המנהל, סורסינג, הערכה וליווי. נגבה לפי מספר המשרות הפתוחות. בחודשים פעילים עם 5 משרות פתוחות או פחות חל מינימום חודשי של 15,000 ש"ח. לדוגמה: 8 משרות פתוחות = 24,000 ש"ח בחודש.
3,000 ש"ח, מינימום 15,000 ש"ח בחודש
דמי השמה לגיוסמשולמים אחרי שהעובד השלים חודשיים מלאים בתפקיד. הריטיינר ששולם מקוזז במלואו מדמי ההשמה.
100% ממשכורת ברוטו אחת, בקיזוז הריטיינר
כל שקל ריטיינר מקוזז מדמי ההשמה, ודמי ההשמה לעולם לא יעלו על משכורת ברוטו אחת לגיוס
דוגמה: בתקופה עם 6 משרות פתוחות, משרה אוישה אחרי חודשיים וחצי. הריטיינר ששולם עליה: 7,500 ש"ח. העובד נקלט בשכר 42,000 ש"ח ברוטו והשלים חודשיים. דמי ההשמה: 34,500 ש"ח. סך הכול על המשרה: 42,000 ש"ח, בדיוק משכורת אחת.

The gap

Your outsourcing vendor is excellent at employment. It was never built to find and evaluate AI-focused engineering talent

Payroll, benefits, employment at scale: your current vendor does that well, and nothing here touches it. But it was not built to tell a strong software engineer with real AI depth from a well-written CV. And it is not a white-glove service either: deep conversations with your tech team, and speaking to candidates in an engineer's language, were simply never part of that model. The result is familiar: noisy pipelines, weak shortlists, wasted interview hours for hiring managers, long time-to-fill, and mis-hires that surface only after onboarding.

The service

From intake to recommendation: five stages, one clear deliverable

1

Deep intake with the hiring manager

Not just role requirements: the needs of the manager, the squad and the department, including a culture-fit profile for the specific team. All of it encoded into the sourcing and evaluation profile.

2

Role-calibrated sourcing

Software engineers with real AI depth: the right sourcing channels, and outreach that speaks their language.

3

Structured evaluation

Evidence-based scoring across six dimensions, including CV-to-LinkedIn consistency and detection of AI-inflated resumes. Not keyword matching.

4

A shortlist of 3 to 5 finalists

A dossier per finalist: strengths, risks, and what to probe in the interview. The shortlist reflects your team's profile, not a generic search.

5

Interview prep and decision support

A personal interview kit per candidate: questions, listen-for cues and red flags. Transcript analysis and a reasoned recommendation: advance, re-test, or pass.

Commercial model

A retainer that reflects partnership, a placement fee that reflects results

Monthly retainer per open positionCovers the ongoing work inside the team: intake with the manager, sourcing, evaluation and enablement. Charged per open position. In active months with 5 or fewer open positions, a monthly minimum of 15,000 NIS applies. For example: 8 open positions = 24,000 NIS per month.
3,000 NIS, minimum 15,000 NIS per month
Placement fee per hirePaid after the hire completes two full months in the role. All retainer paid is fully deducted from the placement fee.
100% of one month gross salary, minus retainer
Every shekel of retainer is deducted from the placement fee, and the placement fee never exceeds one month gross salary per hire
Example: in a period with 6 open positions, a position fills after two and a half months. Retainer paid on it: 7,500 NIS. The hire starts at 42,000 NIS gross and completes two months. Placement fee: 34,500 NIS. Total for the position: 42,000 NIS, exactly one salary.
