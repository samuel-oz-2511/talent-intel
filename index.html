הפער

מערך מיקור החוץ שלכם מצוין בהעסקה. הוא לא נבנה לאיתור ולהערכה של טאלנט פיתוח עם עומק AI

שכר, תנאים, ניהול העסקה בקנה מידה גדול: את זה הספק הקיים עושה טוב, ואין סיבה לגעת בזה. אבל הוא לא נבנה כדי להבדיל בין מהנדס תוכנה חזק עם עומק AI אמיתי לבין קורות חיים מנוסחים היטב. התוצאה מוכרת: פייפליין רועש, שורטליסטים חלשים, שעות ראיונות מבוזבזות של מנהלים מגייסים, זמן איוש ארוך וטעויות גיוס שמתגלות רק אחרי הקליטה.

The gap

Payroll, benefits, employment at scale: your current vendor does that well, and nothing here touches it. But it was not built to tell a strong software engineer with real AI depth from a well-written CV. The result is familiar: noisy pipelines, weak shortlists, wasted interview hours for hiring managers, long time-to-fill, and mis-hires that surface only after onboarding.

I work side by side with your hiring managers: learning the needs of each manager, each squad and each department, and translating them into a precise sourcing and evaluation profile. The shortlists are a product of that work, not of a generic search.

The service

From intake to recommendation: five stages, one clear deliverable

1

Deep intake with the hiring manager

Not just role requirements: the needs of the manager, the squad and the department, including a culture-fit profile for the specific team. All of it encoded into the sourcing and evaluation profile.

2

Role-calibrated sourcing

Software engineers with real AI depth: the right sourcing channels, and outreach that speaks their language.

3

Structured evaluation

Evidence-based scoring across six dimensions, including CV-to-LinkedIn consistency and detection of AI-inflated resumes. Not keyword matching.

4

A shortlist of 3 to 5 finalists

A dossier per finalist: strengths, risks, and what to probe in the interview. The shortlist reflects your team's profile, not a generic search.

5

Interview prep and decision support

A personal interview kit per candidate: questions, listen-for cues and red flags. Transcript analysis and a reasoned recommendation: advance, re-test, or pass.

Team fit

Every team is a small culture. I help managers define it: what works for them, who succeeded before and why. The evaluation system is calibrated on managers' real past decisions and learns their judgment round after round, and every candidate dossier includes culture-fit and teamwork dimensions backed by interview evidence. Not gut feeling.
