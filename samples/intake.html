  • ניסיון מוכח במערכות רגישות ל-latency

Hiring manager addendum · Hard gates

The following three requirements are non-negotiable. A candidate missing any one of them is not a fit for this position, regardless of how strong they are elsewhere.
  • Ownership of a live SaaS production system, including 24/7 on-call
  • Proven experience with latency-sensitive systems
  • Scale: hundreds of thousands of users and up

Manager taste · Standing remarks

Collected in calibration sessions and updated throughout the process. A manager's remark overrides the soft weighting, unless a requirement is marked as a hard gate.

"Founding-team energy matters more to me than perfect Python." VP R&D · June 2026
"I am wary of people who only see the output and not the people behind the work." CEO · June 2026

Soft signals to listen for

  • Bias to ship early and learn from feedback
  • Learning loop: what they changed after a real mistake
  • Low-ego collaboration, gives credit to others

Leadership lens · Gates and weights

  • GATE G1  Willingness to work on-site most weekdays
  • GATE G2  Real depth in server-side Python
  • GATE G3  Startup or founding-team exposure

Weighted positives

  • P1  Built a real-time system from scratch HIGH weight · VP R&D
  • P2  Moved from startup to scale-up and survived the transition MEDIUM weight · CEO
  • P3  Open-source contribution in infrastructure LOW weight

Verdict scale

strong_yes = 100 · lean_yes = 70 · lean_no = 35 · no = 0

Explicitly not encoded

Home location, family status, gender and university are kept out of the evaluation system, for equal-opportunity reasons. The evaluation rests on professional evidence only.
